Overview

The Disaster Response Observation Network (DRON) is a proof-of-concept initiative that aims to leverage unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to gather intelligence during structural fires to aide first responders in their scene assessment and emergency response.

Impact

  • Directing a team of 5 engineers to develop a robust and modular 3D printed mechanical design of an autonomous drone swarm for disaster response
  • Developing custom airframes in SolidWorks, fabricated in-house using 3D printing, incorporating carbon fiber tubes, and integrating electronics and novel sensors
  • Initiated an iterative 3D printing prototyping process to rapidly incorporate learned improvements, resulting in a collaborative agile design cycle
